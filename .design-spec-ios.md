## Glass card (the universal card)
background: rgba(255,255,255,0.04–0.06) + backdropFilter blur(24px) (ultraThinMaterial),
border: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.08), borderRadius: 20 (also 16/18/22/24/26),
boxShadow: 0 8px 24px rgba(0,255,136,0.18) (glow color varies per card)

## Buttons
- Primary CTA: capsule (borderRadius 999), solid #00FF88 (or gradient #00FF88→#88FF00), BLACK text,
  font 15–17 weight 800–900 rounded, heights 42/44/46/48/50/52/54/56/58, glow shadow rgba(0,255,136,.4–.55) blur 10–24
- Secondary: capsule ultraThinMaterial + 1px rgba(255,255,255,0.1–0.16) border, white text
- Tinted pill: bg tint@0.10–0.14, border tint@0.3–0.45 1px, text tint (e.g. "View Full Statement")
- Press feel: scale(0.93–0.97) + brightness(.95), spring ~220ms
