# PROJECT: Official landing page for BlinkWorld — blinkworld.xyz

You are designing the official marketing website for BlinkWorld, a real-world
adventure game launching soon on iPhone and Android. IMPORTANT CONTEXT: Apple
App Review will visit this website as the app's marketing and support URL, so
it must be polished, honest, family-friendly, and 100% clean. Build one
beautiful landing page plus three utility pages (/privacy, /terms, /support).

──────────────────────────────────────────
1. WHAT BLINKWORLD IS (source of truth — never invent features)
──────────────────────────────────────────
BlinkWorld turns real streets, parks, and sidewalks into a living treasure
map. Players walk around in the real world to:
- Follow a live map to grab glowing Blink Orbs, crack open hidden treasure
chests, uncover supply drops, and dig up mystery geodes
- Catch 60+ fantasy creatures through a cinematic AR camera experience, each
with a personality and a rarity tier (Common, Uncommon, Rare, Legendary, Mythic)
- Customize their own explorer character AND a cute companion pet (cat or dog)
that walks beside them on the map
- Build daily walk streaks — steps and workouts (opt-in Apple Health) unlock
bonus Blink Orbs with fair daily caps
- Watch a Live World Feed of catches and milestone moments from explorers
everywhere (locations always privacy-blurred) and send Cheers
- Battle friends' creatures, team up in co-op events, and place caught
creatures into the real world for AR selfies in AR Studio
Official tagline: "Walk. Catch. Explore. Battle."

──────────────────────────────────────────
2. BRAND & VISUAL DESIGN
──────────────────────────────────────────
- Dark, premium, game-grade aesthetic. Page background #0A0A0F, elevated
card surfaces #12121A.
- Primary accent: electric neon green #00FF88. Secondary accent: lime #88FF00.
Text: pure white, white at 70%, white at 50%.
- Rarity color palette for small accents: Common #9AA3B2, Uncommon #00FF88,
Rare #88FF00, Legendary #FFD166, Mythic #FF8AE0.
- Glassmorphism everywhere: frosted translucent cards, 20px rounded corners,
1px white border at 8% opacity, soft green glow shadows.
- Atmosphere: subtle radial green auroras, tiny floating glowing orbs, a faint
map-grid texture behind the hero. Never flat, boring solid sections.
- Typography: a modern geometric sans-serif with a friendly-but-premium feel.
Huge confident headlines, generous line spacing.
- Motion: sections fade-and-rise into view on scroll (staggered), glows pulse
slowly like breathing, creature art floats gently, buttons subtly scale on
press. Respect the user's reduced-motion setting.
- Mobile-first and fully responsive — must look flawless on a phone.

──────────────────────────────────────────
5. HARD COMPLIANCE RULES — ZERO EXCEPTIONS
──────────────────────────────────────────
- NEVER use these words or concepts anywhere on the site (including copy,
alt text, image filenames, and meta tags): crypto, cryptocurrency, token,
coin, $BLINK, blockchain, NFT, web3, wallet, mining, staking, airdrop,
cash out, withdraw, payout, earn money, real money, dollar amounts, USD,
invest, trading, gambling, jackpot, casino, betting, lottery, cash prizes.
- Blink Orbs are ONLY ever described as in-game collectible points for fun.
Never currency, never rewards with value, never redeemable for anything
outside the game. Prefer the word "collect" over "earn" everywhere.
- No "get paid to walk" or income claims of any kind.
- No medical or weight-loss claims. "Get outside and move" motivation is fine.
- No fake press quotes, fake user counts, fake ratings, or fake testimonials.
- Do not use Apple or Google logos or recreate their store badges — custom
text chips only, until launch.
- Only describe the features listed in section 1. Do not invent features,
launch dates, or partnerships.
- Accessible: alt text on all images, keyboard navigable, strong contrast.
